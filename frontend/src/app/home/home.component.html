DISCOVER THE WORLD OF FROSTLINE

Challenge yourself to recognize iconic locations from the Frostline map. How well do you know the terrain?

About Dayz Guesser

"DayZGuessr is an immersive game that tests your knowledge of the DayZ map. Whether you're a veteran or a novice explorer, each game takes you to the very heart of the world and atmosphere of DayZ."

"With over NaN locations to discover, you'll never cease to be amazed by the details scattered across the entire FrostLine map."

Features :

- 5 timed rounds with 30 seconds per round.
- Scoring based on the proximity of your guess.
- A collapsible map for an immersive experience.
